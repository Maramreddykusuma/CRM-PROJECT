Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions?
Here you'll find the answers most valued by our partners!!

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, select the items you want, and proceed to checkout.

2. Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Contact our customer support for assistance.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary.

6. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused and unopened items. Please refer to our Returns page for more information.

7. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at fashionhub@gmail.com or by phone at +91123456789 .

8. Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all of our products are cruelty-free and not tested on animals.

9. Can I change or update my shipping address?

If your order has not been shipped yet, you can contact us to update your shipping address. Once the order is shipped, we cannot change the address.

10. Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee. You can select this option during checkout.
